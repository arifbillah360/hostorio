Domain FAQs

Common questions about domain registration

What is a domain name?

A domain name is your website's address on the internet. It's what people type in their browser to visit your website (e.g., www.yourbusiness.com). Think of it as your online street address.

How long does domain registration take?

Domain registration is instant! Once you complete the registration process and payment, your domain is activated immediately and ready to use.

Can I transfer my domain later?

Yes, you can transfer your domain to another registrar after 60 days from registration. We make the transfer process simple and provide full support.

Do I need hosting to register a domain?

No, you can register a domain without purchasing hosting. However, to launch a website, you'll need both a domain and hosting. We offer bundled packages for better value.

What happens when my domain expires?

You'll receive renewal reminders before expiration. After expiration, there's a grace period where you can renew. If not renewed, the domain enters a redemption period before becoming available for others to register.

Is domain privacy protection included?

Yes! We provide free domain privacy protection (WHOIS privacy) for eligible domain extensions to protect your personal information from public WHOIS databases.
