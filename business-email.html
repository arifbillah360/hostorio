FAQs

What is email hosting?

Just like with web hosting, email hosting is a service provided by a hosting or email company to manage custom domain-based email accounts. Most companies basically rent out space on an email server to customers, then handle email sending and receiving. Our professional email is, however, more than just a storage space and delivery service. It's a wide variety of integrations and collaboration features available to boost productivity and team effectiveness.

Can I find out who is hosting my email right now?

Yes, you can use various online tools to check your email hosting provider by looking at your domain's MX records. Contact our support team if you need help identifying your current email host.

How do I get a company email address?

Simply choose one of our business email plans, complete the registration, and you'll be able to create professional email addresses using your company domain name (e.g., yourname@yourcompany.com).

How do I set up my business email?

After purchasing your email plan, you'll receive setup instructions via email. Our support team is also available 24/7 to help you configure your email client, whether it's on desktop, mobile, or webmail.

What are the benefits of email hosting?

Professional email hosting gives you a branded email address, enhanced security, spam protection, larger storage capacity, collaboration tools, and reliable support - all while maintaining a professional image for your business.

Are email migrations available when switching to Private Email?

Yes! We offer free email migration services. Our technical team will help you transfer all your existing emails, contacts, and calendars from your current provider to Hostorio with zero data loss.
