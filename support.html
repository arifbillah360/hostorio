Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I buy web hosting?

Simply choose your preferred hosting package from our plans, click the "BUY NOW" button, and follow the checkout process. You can pay using various payment methods including bKash, Nagad, or bank transfer.

Which web hosting plan do I need to choose?

It depends on your website type: Standard for portfolios, Professional for blogs, Business for business websites, and Diamond for eCommerce sites. If you're unsure, contact our support team for personalized recommendations.

Can I upgrade my hosting package?

Yes! You can upgrade your hosting package at any time. Simply contact our support team, and we'll help you seamlessly migrate to a higher-tier plan with no downtime.

Do you offer free website migration?

Yes, we offer free website migration services for all new customers. Our technical team will handle the entire transfer process to ensure your website is moved safely with zero downtime.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 30 days, contact us for a full refund, no questions asked.

How do I access my cPanel?

You can access cPanel by visiting yourdomain.com/cpanel or through the client area. Your cPanel login credentials are sent to your email after account activation.

What backup options do you provide?

We provide free daily automated backups for all hosting accounts. You can restore your backups anytime from cPanel. We keep backups for 30 days.

Is SSL certificate included?

Yes, we provide free SSL certificates for all domains hosted with us. SSL certificates are automatically installed and renewed, ensuring your website is always secure.
